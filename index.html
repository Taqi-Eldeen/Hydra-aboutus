Abstract

Intelligent Protection
Beyond Signatures

Traditional WAFs rely on static regex rules — effective against known attack patterns, but fundamentally blind to evolving zero-day threats. OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities continue to cause major security incidents, while signature-based defenses are routinely bypassed by attackers who understand the playbook.

Web-Hydra introduces a 9-Layer Architecture that fuses OWASP CRS signature matching with deep learning-based anomaly detection. Deep Learning Autoencoders learn the statistical profile of normal traffic; CNNs classify threat types with precision. Together they stop the attacks that no rule has ever described.

Deployed as a containerized reverse-proxy, Web-Hydra operates transparently between client and application — delivering immediate, intelligent, and unforgiving protection without modifying existing infrastructure.

Methodology

9-Layer Defense
Architecture

A compartmentalized pipeline processes every HTTP/S request through signature verification, AI anomaly detection, threat intelligence, and unified risk scoring — before a final allow or block decision is enforced.

01

Request Interception

All incoming HTTP/S traffic is captured at the traffic layer before reaching the upstream application — zero-trust from the first byte.

02

Decode & Normalize

URL decoding, header normalization, and payload unpacking transform obfuscated input into a canonical form ready for analysis.

03

Signature Check

OWASP CRS fingerprint matching against thousands of known attack patterns — SQLi, XSS, RCE, SSRF, path traversal, and more.

04

Feature Extraction

Key attributes, n-gram vectors, and statistical features are extracted from request parameters, headers, and body content.

05

Deep Learning Model

Autoencoder-based anomaly detection identifies statistical deviations from learned normal traffic — catching zero-day attacks without any signature.

06

Classification Model

A CNN classifier assigns attack-type labels and confidence scores to flagged requests for precise, explainable threat categorization.

07

Threat Intelligence

Real-time IP/domain reputation lookup enriches each request with external threat-intel context to surface attacker origin and history.

08

Risk Scoring & Policy

A unified scoring engine aggregates all signals into a single risk score, enforcing configurable allow/block thresholds per policy.

09

Log & Dashboard

Every decision is logged with full context. A real-time dashboard surfaces attack trends, behavioral profiles, CVE matches, and patch recommendations.
